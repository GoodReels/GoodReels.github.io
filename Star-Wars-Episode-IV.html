Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope

Description

Luke Skywalker (Mark Hamill) joins forces with a Jedi Knight (Alec Guinness), a cocky pilot (Harrison Ford), a Wookiee (Peter Mayhew) and two droids (Anthony Daniels & Kenny Baker) to save the galaxy from the Empire's world-destroying battle-station, while also attempting to rescue Princess Leia (Carrie Fisher) from the evil Darth Vader (David Prowse, James Earl Jones [voice]).
