The Wizard of Oz



Director:

Victor Fleming

Actors:

Judy Garland
Frank Morgan
Ray Bolger

Release Date:

1939

MPAA Rating:

PG/G

Description:

Dorothy (Judy Garland), a young girl from Kansas, is swept off on an adventure to the mystical land of Oz. Pursued by the Wicked Witch of the West on her quest to find the Wizard and return home, Dorothy forms friendships with Scarecrow, Tin Man, and the Cowardly Lion.
